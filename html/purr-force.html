Purr Force: Scratch & Rescue

Game Designer / Technical Designer

Platform: PC
Engine: Unity 6.1
Team Size: 8
Duration: 5 weeks
Available On Itch.io!

Tools Used:
  • Unity 6.1
  • C#
  • Git LFS
Description:

Purr Force: Scratch & Rescue is a grid-based strategy game about extinguishing wildfires. Control adorable cat firefighters as you cut down trees to create buffer zones, put out wildfires, and bring in the big guns with various abilities. Earn points based on the number of trees and houses saved!

My Role:

As a Game Design Intern at IndieCade, I collaborated with a team of 8 to develop Purr Force: Scratch & Rescue for Climate Jam 2025. My responsibilities included:

  • Collaborated on Game Design Document and designed player abilities systems
  • Developed core gameplay systems including player character controller, camera mechanics, and level design prefabs
  • Handled technical art asset implementation, integrating character models with animations
  • Created UI wireframes and conducted iterative playtesting sessions, implementing usability improvements and gameplay balance adjustments based on user feedback
